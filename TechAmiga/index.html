TechAmiga empowers women in Latin America and the U.S. through technology and mentorship.

Apply to TechAmiga Foundation Why Tech?

TechAmiga is on a mission to connect 10,000 women across Latin America and the U.S. through mentorship and STEAM education.

In Latin America, only about 33% of professional roles in STEAM are held by women, who often face cultural and social barriers that limit their access to education. In the U.S., women make up around 27% of the STEAM workforce and are often looking for ways to share their expertise and mentor others.

TechAmiga bridges this gap by fostering a space where Latin American women can learn from experienced U.S. professionals, enhancing their career skills while promoting meaningful cultural exchange.
